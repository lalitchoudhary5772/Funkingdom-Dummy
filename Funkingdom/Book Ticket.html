Guidlines of the Park

Amusement Guidline

Dress Fitted clothes for Monocycle, Rangers, Pedal Boat, Go-Go Cycle, etc.

Other Important Guidlines

  • Re-Entry is Prohibited i.e. One time Entry & Exit.
  • Guests can store their belongings (Handbags, Mobile & Extra Clothes) in lockers.
  • Outside food & beverages are not allowed in the park.
  • Rides cannot be taken in wet clothes.
  • Shoes are mandatory for adventure activities.
  • Must fill participant waiver form at the entrance.
  • Intoxication including liquor, cigarette, tobacco, are strictly prohibited in the park.
  • Loose attire including Sarees, Plazo, Salwar Suit, Skirts are not allowed to wear in Adventure activity, Monocycle and some of the
    rides.
