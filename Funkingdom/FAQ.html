Frequently Asked Questions

Browse throungh the set of Frequently Asked Questions. Hope these will solve your queries. In case you hold any other queries, kindly feel free
to reach us at: info@funkingdomjaipur.com

When is Fun Kingdom opening ?
Cheer Up, because the park is already operational with various exciting rides. Hurry and book your tickets here.
How much do Fun kingdom Amusement Park tickets cost in 2022?
Kindly visit tickets and offers section on the website for tickets pricing. Pre-book your Fun kingdom tickets.
Do you need to book Fun kingdom tickets in advance?
No, the there is an option to book the tickets both online and offline. However, it is recommended to book your tickets well ahead by
either online or calling us. It will help you save time standing in the queue and give direct entry to the park by simply scanning a
barcode. In case of online bookings, the customers are advised to make the bookings atleast 3 days prior to the visit.
Can we buy tickets at Fun kingdom?
You can buy Fun kingdom tickets at the Park Ticket Counter as well as online. We will update on the website for any further changes.
How can I get a ticket discount at Fun kingdom?
In our ‘Tickets and Offers’ section on the website, you can see currently available deals and discounts.
Do we need to pay extra for amusement park rides?
You can buy the package deal which includes Adventure activities. You will have to pay extra for food, souvenirs.
Will the amusement park Fun kingdom offer group discounts?
Generally we offer deals for multiple categories. You can find the details under the tickets and offers section. Or you can even reach out
to us at the mentioned contact details.
What hotels are near Fun Kingdom ?
There are number of Luxury stays close to Fun kingdom as it is located in the prominent location.
Does Fun Kingdom ticket include food ?
No, it doesn’t. You can Pre-buy food voucher as Add-on and save.
What is the best time to visit Fun Kingdom ?
Fun kingdom is an all-season destination. You can plan your visit as per your comfort for any day in the year.
